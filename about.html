We're The Henrys and we're a family of four. In 2010, Omar and Jereen met, began dating in 2013, and married in 2017. Nathaniel was born in 2018 and Natania, our youngest, was born in 2020. We are a God-fearing Christian family that abide by the motto "No One Gets Left Behind." As a family, we take care of each other. Our date nights usually take place after the kids have gone to bed or while they are at school.

Our goal is to have a good time with each other and spend as much time together as possible, focusing on the present and each other! We hope you will join us on our young couple's adventure. We teach our children to be courteous and attempt to nurture them in a quiet and joyful environment! While also disseminating information on how to build family bonds.
